intro

hey, if you're wondering why someone might be inclined to live with a dumbphone, checkout this post that I wrote.

finding a dumbphone that works with local carriers, one that isn't a test of masochism to use, and one that maintains a "wholesome" feature set, is such a challenge to find that you'd have a better chance understanding Ulysses after a single pass—yet, I think my quest is finally finished.

having been part of the dumbphone community for several years now (yes, it exists)—and having tested dozens of dumbphones, the 903kc has been the best dumbphone I've tested. it maintains a special set of features that allow it to conform well with the digital minimalism lifestyle.
